# Competitor Subscription Model Research

## Key Insight: Nobody uses monthly auto-recurring subscriptions in this space.

All Indian tender platforms sell **fixed-duration access licenses** (1 year, 2 year), NOT monthly subscriptions. 
This is the industry standard for this market.

---

## BidAssist (bidassist.com) — Largest Player

**Tagline:** "Guaranteed value-for-money Plans to Get Latest Tender Documents"
**Trust signals shown upfront:** "No Hidden Charges", "3-days Free Trial for New User", "No Credit Card Required"

### Plan Structure: Fixed Duration (1 Year / 2 Year Toggle)

| Plan | 1 Year | 2 Year | Scope | CTA |
|------|--------|--------|-------|-----|
| Basic | ₹3,000 | ₹5,000 | One State | "Buy Basic" |
| Standard ⭐ | ₹10,000 | ₹14,000 | All India States | "Buy Standard" |
| Professional | Custom | Custom | Premium Features | "Request Call Back" |

### What's Free vs Paid:
- **Free forever:** Search any tender (keyword, location, authority)
- **Free Trial (3 days):** Full access, no credit card
- **Paid Only:** Document downloads, daily email/SMS alerts, archived tenders
- **Pay-per-tender:** ₹299 per tender document (for users who don't subscribe)

### Key UX Patterns:
- 1 Year / 2 Year toggle (NOT monthly/annual)
- "Most Popular Plan" badge on Standard
- "View More Features" expand button under each card
- "Request Call Back" for top tier (no self-serve pricing)
- No monthly billing at all

## Key Takeaways for GeMTenders

### 1. Don't do monthly subscriptions
Nobody in this space does it. Users expect to pay once a year and forget it.

### 2. Feature gating that actually makes sense here:
- **Free:** Search + browse all tenders (you already do this ✅)
- **Paid:** Email Alerts / Phone notifications / WhatsApp alerts
- The pain point is ALERTS, not search. People pay to be notified.

### 3. Recommended Plan Restructure:
| Plan | Price | Key Gate |
|------|-------|---------|
| Free | ₹0 | Search & Browse only, no alerts |
| Starter | ₹999/year | Email alerts + 3 keywords |
| Pro | ₹2,999/year | Unlimited keywords + WhatsApp alerts + AI summaries |

### 4. Trigger = Dropdown filter awareness
When a user uses the Keyword/State filter → show a banner:
"Get notified automatically when new tenders match this search → Upgrade to Starter"

This is much less confusing than presenting a generic subscription page.
